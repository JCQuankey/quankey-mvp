Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about quantum-proof password security

Is it really quantum-proof? +
Yes. Quankey uses true quantum entropy from Australian National University's Quantum Random Number Generator and is ready for post-quantum cryptographic algorithms like CRYSTALS-Kyber. While traditional password managers use pseudo-random generation that quantum computers can predict, our quantum entropy is truly unpredictable.
What if I lose my device? +
That's where Quankey shines. Using quantum-secured recovery shares and Shamir's Secret Sharing, you can recover your entire vault in under 90 seconds from any new device. No master password to remember, no lengthy recovery processes. Your device can be lost, stolen, or completely destroyed - your passwords remain safe and accessible.
How is it faster than traditional password managers? +
Quankey's architecture is built for performance. Our browser extension provides <50ms autofill (2-4x faster than competitors), smart form detection, and optimized quantum algorithms. We've eliminated the bottlenecks that slow down traditional password managers while adding quantum security.
Why no master password? +
Master passwords are the weakest link in password security. They can be forgotten, stolen, or compromised. Quankey uses biometric authentication (fingerprint/face) combined with quantum-secured recovery shares. This means even if someone has your fingerprint, they can't access your vault without your device and recovery shares.
