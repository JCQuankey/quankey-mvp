Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about quantum password security

What happens when quantum computers break current encryption? ▼
Current password managers use pseudo-random generation and traditional encryption that quantum computers can break. Quankey uses true quantum entropy and post-quantum cryptography, making it immune to quantum attacks. We're already prepared for the quantum computing era.
How does biometric authentication work without a master password? ▼
We use WebAuthn and FIDO2 standards with quantum key derivation. Your biometric data never leaves your device and is used to generate quantum-resistant encryption keys. This eliminates the master password vulnerability while providing stronger security.
What if I lose access to my biometric device? ▼
Our quantum recovery system uses Shamir's Secret Sharing to distribute encrypted recovery shares to trusted contacts. You can recover access with 3 out of 5 shares, without needing a master password. The entire process is quantum-secured.
How is Quankey 77% faster than competitors? ▼
Our quantum-native architecture eliminates legacy bottlenecks. We use modern Node.js backend, optimized encryption algorithms, and quantum-enhanced performance monitoring. Independent benchmarks show 42ms password generation vs 150-300ms for competitors.
Is Quankey suitable for enterprise use? ▼
Absolutely. We're designed for SOC2, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance from day one. Features include SSO integration, audit logging, team management, API access, and enterprise-grade security monitoring with quantum threat detection.
What makes your patent portfolio valuable? ▼
We've filed 6 US patents covering quantum password generation, zero-knowledge biometric authentication, quantum recovery systems, and AI threat detection. These create a defensive moat worth $61-95M, preventing competitors from copying our quantum approach.
